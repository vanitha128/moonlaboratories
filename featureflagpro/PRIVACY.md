# FeatureFlagPro Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-08

This Privacy Policy explains how Moon Labs ("we", "us", "our") handles data
when you use the FeatureFlagPro plugin ("Plugin").

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to the FeatureFlagPro JetBrains plugin and related support
interactions.

## 2. Data Processing Overview

FeatureFlagPro is designed to process project source files and configuration
primarily within your local IDE environment.

- We do not operate a backend that ingests your source code by default.
- We do not intentionally collect your project code on Moon Labs servers.

## 3. Data You Provide in Plugin Settings

Depending on configuration, you may enter credentials or identifiers for third-
party feature flag providers (for example API tokens, project keys, namespace
IDs, URLs).

- These values are stored in your local IDE configuration.
- These values are used by the Plugin to call provider APIs that you configure.

You are responsible for managing those credentials and following your
organization's security policies.

## 4. Network Calls

The Plugin may send network requests only to:

- feature flag providers and endpoints you explicitly configure;
- JetBrains Marketplace infrastructure for plugin licensing and billing;
- JetBrains platform services required for plugin operation.

We do not sell personal data.

## 5. Third-Party Services

When you connect FeatureFlagPro to third-party providers, your use of those
services is governed by their own terms and privacy policies. We are not
responsible for third-party privacy or security practices.

## 6. Logs and Diagnostics

The Plugin may write logs to your local IDE log files for troubleshooting.
Those logs can include error messages and technical metadata. Avoid sharing
logs publicly if they may contain sensitive environment details.

If you contact support and choose to share logs or screenshots, you consent to
our use of that information solely to provide support and improve reliability.

## 7. Data Retention

Plugin configuration and logs remain in your local environment until removed by
you or your system policies.

Support emails and attachments are retained only as long as reasonably
necessary for support, legal compliance, and record-keeping.

## 8. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect support
communications and operational data under our control. No method of storage or
transmission is completely secure.

## 9. Children's Privacy

FeatureFlagPro is intended for professional developers and is not directed to
children.

## 10. International Use

If you use the Plugin from outside Australia, you understand that support data
you voluntarily send may be processed in jurisdictions where we or our service
providers operate.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be
published with the Plugin materials or repository documentation.

## 12. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact:

- Raise support request in https://product.moonlaboratories.com/support
- Vendor: Moon Laboratories

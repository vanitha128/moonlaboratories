# FeatureFlagPro Support Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-08

This Support Policy describes how Moon Labs provides support for the
FeatureFlagPro plugin.

## 1. Support Channels

- Primary email: support@moonlabs.dev
- Please include: IDE version, plugin version, operating system, and clear
  reproduction steps.

## 2. Supported Product Scope

Support covers:

- installation and activation issues;
- plugin configuration and provider connection setup;
- scanning, navigation, and dashboard behavior;
- reproducible bugs and regressions in released versions.

Support does not include:

- custom development for private workflows;
- debugging third-party services unrelated to plugin behavior;
- issues caused by unsupported IDE builds or modified environments.

## 3. Eligibility

- Paid subscribers receive standard commercial support.
- Trial users may receive best-effort support.
- Free or read-only tiers (if offered) are handled on a best-effort basis.

## 4. Response Targets

Business hours: Monday to Friday, 9:00 to 17:00 (AEST/AEDT), excluding public
holidays in New South Wales, Australia.

Target first-response times:

- Critical issue (plugin unusable): within 1 business day
- High issue (major feature blocked): within 2 business days
- Normal issue (non-blocking bug/question): within 3 business days

These are targets, not guaranteed service levels.

## 5. Issue Severity Guidelines

- Critical: plugin cannot start or primary workflow is completely blocked.
- High: major functionality broken with no practical workaround.
- Normal: partial impact, workaround exists, or general guidance request.

## 6. What to Include in a Support Request

To speed resolution, include:

- JetBrains IDE name and version;
- FeatureFlagPro version;
- operating system and version;
- exact steps to reproduce;
- expected behavior vs actual behavior;
- relevant logs or screenshots (redacted for secrets).

## 7. Security and Sensitive Data

Do not send secrets in plain text unless strictly required. If logs include
tokens, passwords, or internal URLs, redact them before sharing.

## 8. Update and Fix Policy

Bug fixes may be delivered in patch or minor plugin releases. We prioritize
issues by severity, impact, reproducibility, and customer reach.

## 9. End-of-Life and Compatibility

We aim to support current JetBrains platform versions compatible with the
plugin's declared build range. Older IDE versions may receive limited or no
support.

## 10. Contact

Support contact:

- https://product.moonlaboratories.com/support

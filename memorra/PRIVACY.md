# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Memorra (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information.

This Privacy Policy explains how Memorra handles information when you use our mobile application and related services.

## 1. Overview

Memorra is designed as a local-first application.

Your reminders, assets, attachments, and personal information are stored locally on your device unless explicitly stated otherwise in future features.

We aim to collect as little personal data as possible.

---

## 2. Information Stored on Your Device

Memorra may store the following information locally on your device:

- Assets you create (pets, vehicles, home items, subscriptions, documents, etc.)
- Reminder details and schedules
- Notes and attachments you add
- App preferences and settings
- Notification schedules

This information is stored locally using on-device databases and file storage.

---

## 3. Notifications

Memorra may request permission to send local notifications for reminders and recurring events.

Notification scheduling occurs locally on your device.

We do not send reminder information to external servers.

---

## 4. Attachments and Files

If you add files, photos, or documents to Memorra:

- Files are stored locally on your device
- Files remain under your control
- Memorra does not upload attachments to external servers in the current version

Deleting the app may remove locally stored attachments unless backed up externally by you or your device backup system.

---

## 5. Analytics and Diagnostics

Current versions of Memorra may collect limited anonymous diagnostic information such as:

- crash reports
- performance diagnostics
- app stability metrics

This information may be collected through Apple or third-party crash reporting tools to improve app stability and reliability.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal reminder content for analytics purposes.

---

## 6. No Account Required

Memorra currently does not require account creation.

You may use the app without providing:
- name
- email address
- phone number

Future optional cloud-sync features may require account creation. If introduced, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

---

## 7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share your personal reminder data with advertisers.

We may share limited technical information with service providers that help us:
- maintain app stability
- process diagnostics
- improve reliability

These providers are required to protect information appropriately.

---

## 8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information stored within the app.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

---

## 9. Children’s Privacy

Memorra is not directed toward children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 10. Your Control

Because Memorra stores most information locally on your device, you generally control your data directly.

You may:
- edit your information
- delete reminders
- remove attachments
- delete all local data from within the app
- uninstall the app at any time

---

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Updated versions will be posted within the app or associated website.

Continued use of Memorra after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
